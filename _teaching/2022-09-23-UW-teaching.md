Teaching undergraduate physics courses in both lab and tutorial settings, responsible for
assisting students, facilitating group work, grading, and creating exam questions, solutions,
and rubrics. Topics include electromagnetism, waves and optics, classical kinematics, lab
safety, research skill building and best practices for academic writing. 

**PHYS121 Introductory Classical Mechanics** (Winter 2023)
---
Topics included: One dimensional motion, Momentum, Energy, Motion in two or more dimensions, Rotational motion, Gravity

**PHYS122 Introductory Electromagnetism** (Fall 2022, Fall 2023, Spring 2025)
---
Topics included: Electric and magnetic interactions, Electric circuits, Electromagnetic waves


**PHYS123 Introductory Waves and Optics** (Spring 2023)
---
Topics included: Simple harmonic rotation, Wave propagation, Wave interference and diffraction, Optics, Heat transfer, Converting heat to work


**PHYS321 Electrostatics** (Spring 2024, Fall 2024)
---
Topics included: Develop a basic familiarity with classical field theory, with a focus on scalar fields and curl-free vector fields, develop an intuition for static electric fields and electric potentials in vacuum and in matter, develop a toolkit of mathematical skills for manipulating scalar and vector fields and solving related problems


**PHYS322 Electromagnetism II** (Winter 2025)
---
Topics included: Charges at rest and in motion, dielectric and magnetic media, electromagnetic waves, relativity and electromagnetism, physical optics

---

**PHYS329 Mathematical Methods of Classical Mechanics** (Spring 2025)
Topics included: Mathematical methods applied to classical mechanics, Lagrangian mechanics, gravity, orbits
