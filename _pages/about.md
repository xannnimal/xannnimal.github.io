Hi, I'm Xan! 

I am a postdoctural researcher in the [Gwilliams Laboratory for Speech Neurosciance](https://gwilliamslab.stanford.edu) at Stanford University. I recieved my PhD in Applied Physics at the University of Washington, Seattle where I developed improvements to the methodology and instrumentation for on-scalp MEG, with a focus on OPM-MEG systems. At Stanford, I am continuing my work with MEG to further the study of speech and cognitive neuroscience with non-invasive brain imaging techniques. 

When I am not researching, I am outside exploring or inside watercolor painting. See my work in the Portfolio tab!
